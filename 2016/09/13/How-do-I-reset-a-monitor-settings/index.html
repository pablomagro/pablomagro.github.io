How do I reset a monitor settings?

KDE4

Remove the folder ~/.kde/share/apps/kscreen/ or ~/.kde4/share/apps/kscreen/ (depending on your distribution) for KDE4.

Plasma 5

Remove the folder ~/.local/share/kscreen/ for Plasma 5.
